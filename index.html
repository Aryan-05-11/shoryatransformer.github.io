Custom Transformer Design and Development

Customized transformer solutions are built according to client specifications, technical requirements, product application, and production needs. The team can discuss samples, size constraints, winding details, and performance expectations before manufacturing.

Bulk Production and Supply

Shorya Transformer can fulfill small to large-scale industrial orders with consistent attention to material handling, winding work, assembly, checking, packing, and dispatch schedules.

How Orders Move

A clear process for custom and bulk electronic transformer orders.

Step 1

Requirement Discussion

Share the transformer type, rating, size, quantity, application, photos, sample details or drawing.

Step 2

Technical Confirmation

The requirement is reviewed so the team can understand winding, core, output and usage expectations.

Step 3

Manufacturing

Production work begins with material preparation, winding, assembly and order-based handling.

Step 4

Quality Check

Products are checked before dispatch to support stable performance and reduce avoidable issues.

Step 5

Packing and Dispatch

Orders are packed and supplied to customers in Delhi NCR and across India as discussed.
